Frequently Asked Questions

1. What’s included in a regular cleaning service?

Regular cleaning includes dusting, vacuuming, mopping, bathroom cleaning, and kitchen surface wiping.

2. Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

No, you don’t need to be home. Many clients provide access instructions for our trusted team.

3. Do you bring your own supplies and equipment?

Yes, we bring all necessary eco-friendly products and professional equipment.

4. How long does a cleaning session usually take?

It depends on the size and condition of your space, but most cleanings take 2 to 3 hours.

5. Can I schedule recurring cleanings?

Absolutely! We offer weekly, biweekly, and monthly cleaning plans.

6. What if I’m not satisfied with the service?

Your satisfaction is our priority. If anything is missed, we’ll come back and make it right at no extra cost.
